About Us
by Imaya Dismas

Welcome !

One way to learn about peoples and cultures is through literature, their stories, novels, poetry and plays. Through these things we can get at least glimpses, and in many cases, pretty clear pictures, of the lives and thought worlds of groups of people.

Our Vision

All African people in underdeveloped and developing countries have similar access to literature facilities as those in developed countries.

Our Mission

Our core mission is to promote literature development in Africa by providing literature resources from African Authors.

What We Do

We gather literature resources from institutional and individual authors in Africa and make them available for all people.
